The story behind the name

My brother was one of the most respected DJs of his generation — a man who understood that music doesn't just fill a room. It transforms it. He was born in Germany, took his music to every corner of the world, and was my best friend until I lost him in 2020. Haus is German for house — a nod to where he began.

Coleman is our mother's name. I was building this brand in secret, waiting until it was ready — until I could walk in and show her something whole, something worthy of carrying her name. She passed 25 days after I started, before I ever got the chance to tell her. She never knew it existed.

But I built it for them. And I carry them both into every space I design — the music, the memory, the frequency that never really goes away.

That is what Haus of Coleman is built on.
